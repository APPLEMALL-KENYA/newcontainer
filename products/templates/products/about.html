About Us

Container Spaces Kenya is a dynamic container brokerage and logistics company dedicated to connecting businesses with reliable, cost-effective container solutions. Based in Kenya, we specialize in sourcing, leasing, and selling shipping containers, as well as providing end-to-end logistics support for exporters, construction firms, NGOs, and SMEs across East Africa.

We pride ourselves on transparency, personalized service, and delivering timely solutions that meet our clients’ diverse storage, transport, and modification needs.
